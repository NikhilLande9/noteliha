02

Description

noteliha is a lightweight and privacy-focused note-taking app designed to help you quickly create, organize, and manage notes. Notes are stored locally on your device and can optionally be backed up to your personal Google Drive account when you choose to. The app focuses on simplicity, speed, and giving you full control over your data.
03

Key Features

Multiple Note Types
  • Regular notes
  • Checklists
  • Recipes
  • Meal plans
  • Travel itineraries
Fast Search

Search by title, content, or category. Results appear instantly while typing.

Smart Organization
  • Pin important notes to the top
  • Organize with categories — General, Work, Personal, Ideas
  • Attach images to notes
  • Soft delete with recycle bin
  • Restore deleted notes anytime
Optional Google Drive Backup
  • Backup enabled only when you sign in and trigger it manually
  • Notes and images stored in a dedicated .liha_notes_app folder
  • Only files created by the app are accessible
  • Manual backup and restore — you stay in control
Offline First
  • Create and edit notes without internet access
  • Sync to Drive manually when you choose to
  • Minimal battery and background usage

05

Privacy & Permissions

Privacy
  • Notes stored locally on your device
  • Optional Google Drive backup under your full control
  • No advertising, no behavioral tracking, no analytics SDKs
Permissions Explained
  • Google Sign-In — used to enable optional Google Drive backup
  • Google Drive Access — allows the app to create and manage its own backup files only
  • Photos — required only when attaching images to notes
  • Local Storage — used to store notes and attachments on the device

The app uses the drive.file OAuth scope only. It cannot access any other files in your Google Drive.

Reviewer Instructions

  1. Create a note with a title and content
  2. Edit the note and add additional content
  3. Use search to find the note
  4. Delete the note and restore it from the recycle bin
  5. Sign in with Google and tap Upload Backup — verify the .liha_notes_app folder is created in Google Drive
  6. Tap Restore from Drive and verify notes are restored correctly
