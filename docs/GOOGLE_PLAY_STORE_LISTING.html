Full Description
Tagline
Your notes. Your privacy. Your control.
Overview
noteliha is a fast, lightweight, and privacy-focused note-taking app designed to keep your notes simple, organized, and secure. Create notes instantly, organize them your way, and optionally back them up to your personal Google Drive. Your notes remain under your control. No tracking. No ads. No unnecessary permissions.

Key Features

5 Note Types
  • Regular notes

Smart Organization
  • Pin important notes to the top
  • Categorize notes — General, Work, Personal, Ideas
  • Soft-delete with recycle bin
  • Restore deleted notes anytime
  • Attach images to notes
Fast Search
  • Search by title, content, or category
  • Instant results while typing
  • Results organized by pinned and recent notes
Secure Cloud Backup
  • Uses Google Drive drive.file permission only
  • Only accesses files created by noteliha
  • Individual note and image files stored in a dedicated folder
  • Manual backup and restore — you decide when to sync
  • Your existing Drive files remain completely inaccessible to the app
Offline First
  • All notes stored locally on your device
  • Create and edit notes without internet access
  • Sync to Drive manually when you choose to

04

Privacy & Permissions

Privacy First
  • Notes stored locally on your device
  • Optional backup to your personal Google Drive only
  • No ads, no analytics, no third-party data sharing
Permissions Explained
  • Google Sign-In — used only when enabling Google Drive backup
  • Google Drive Access — allows the app to create and manage its own backup files only
  • Photos — required only when attaching images to notes
  • Storage Access — used to store notes and attachments locally

Backup files are transmitted via HTTPS and stored only inside the app's dedicated .liha_notes_app folder. No personal data is sold or shared.

Reviewer Instructions

  1. Open the app and create a note with a title and content
  2. Use search to find the note
  3. Pin the note to the top
  4. Delete the note and restore it from the recycle bin
  5. Sign in with Google, tap Upload Backup, and verify the .liha_notes_app folder appears in Google Drive
  6. Tap Restore from Drive and verify notes are restored correctly
