About

noteliha is a fast, offline-first note-taking app for Android and iOS, built by Nikhil Lande at Navkon Labs. It's designed around one idea: your notes belong to you. There are no ads, no trackers, and no accounts required. Notes are stored locally on your device, with optional Google Drive backup entirely under your control. Whether you're jotting quick thoughts, planning a trip, managing a meal plan, or keeping a checklist — noteliha keeps it private, fast, and free.
